My Background

I am a student in MART 145 Web Design, where I have explored the fundamentals of visual design, HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap. Throughout the course, I have learned how to build structured, responsive, and visually engaging web pages. I enjoy applying design concepts to create websites that look clean, organized, and easy to navigate.

My Interests

Beyond web design, I enjoy working with technology, photography, and digital creativity. I like experimenting with color, layout, and motion to bring ideas to life online. I also enjoy learning new tools that help me expand my design abilities.

What I Learned in MART 145

This course helped me develop a strong understanding of coding structure, accessibility, folder organization, and responsive design. It gave me hands-on practice creating real projects that demonstrate my growth.

My Goals

As I continue building my portfolio, I want to explore more advanced interactive elements such as animations, jQuery effects, and dynamic layouts. My goal is to keep improving my design skills and build professional-quality projects. I want to be able to continue the process of combining all my work into fun interactive unique experiences.
